Student Bio

Andrea Grant

Born in Indianapolis, I currently call Grand Rapids home. I went to school at Aquinas College
and majored in English and history. I also studied classical archaeology and studied in Tuscany
with the University of Massachusetts Amherst on their field school dig. I'm still very passionate
about archaeology and love to keep up with the latest finds and developments.

Besides archaeology, I enjoy Irish dance and reading. I started Irish dancing when I was
in high school with the Ardan Academy out of Detroit. Now I dance with the Grand Rapids
based Scoil Rince Ni Bhraonain school, and have done for two years. My reading interests
include science fiction and anything that has to do with history.
